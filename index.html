  • Whisper

    Use whispers to share your experiences with the world in a simple, honest way. Whisper is a social media app where users can create an account and make whispers about their life experiences, thoughts or whatever they want. Other users can view your whispers depending on whether you decide to make them public or private. Marking a whispers as private keeps it from being seen by anyone other than you (even if they follow you). The goal of the app is to encourage honesty, not validation. The lack of commenting and liking on whispers will help an individual speak their mind without fear of what other's think through the number of likes and comments. Have fun and keep it real!

    Tech used: Express, Node, JavaScript, Handlebars, Mongoose, Google-oauth20, Tailwind, Materialize
